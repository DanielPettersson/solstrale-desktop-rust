# Product Guidelines

## Tone and Prose
- **Professional and Technical:** Focus on precision and technical accuracy for experienced users.

## Visual Identity
- **Modern and High-Contrast:** Use a dark theme by default to make rendered images pop.
- **Clean and Uncluttered:** Prioritize the viewport and YAML editor, hiding secondary controls when not needed.

## User Feedback and Error Handling
- **Informative and Actionable:** Provide clear error messages with suggestions on how to fix YAML syntax or rendering issues.
